# BeLive Studios — Brand Guide

---

## Brand Overview

BeLive Studios is the content and IP engine of a vertically integrated content and distribution ecosystem. It is a full-stack IP studio focused on storytelling, microdrama creation, cinematic production, and agile content output — built for high-frequency delivery across OTT and short-form platforms.

**Industry:** Digital content production, IP creation, microdrama, short-form storytelling

**Strategic positioning:** Category-defining — not just a production studio, but the creative core of an OTT-native content ecosystem where IP creation, storytelling, and distribution are fully integrated, data-driven, and AI-augmented.

**Key differentiator:** A full-stack IP studio with strong capabilities in microdrama development, cinematic storytelling, and agile production — operating in tandem with Yeon Studios' OTT infrastructure for tighter control over distribution, data, and monetisation.

---

## Brand Voice & Tone

**How BeLive Studios sounds:** Cinematic, emotionally driven, and culturally sharp.

- Speaks in stories, emotion, and cultural relevance — not specs
- Positions as the creative force behind IP that connects with audiences
- Bold and confident, with cultural awareness
- Future-facing without losing human warmth
- Category-defining: introduces new language for a new format

**Tone keywords:** Cinematic · Emotionally Driven · Culturally Sharp · Bold · Creative

**Off-limits:** Political content, sensitive claims, unverified client results, competitor attacks, off-brand tone (overly corporate, dry, technical).

---

## Target Audience

**Primary targets (outreach):**
- Brand managers and marketing leads seeking content partnerships
- Agency heads and creative directors
- Event organizers in entertainment and media
- Journalists and press covering content, IP, and streaming
- OTT platform operators seeking original IP
- Potential creative collaborators and co-producers

**Market:**
- Primary: Singapore (strategic base and innovation hub)
- Secondary: Southeast Asia
- Expansion: Korea, China, global via IP and distribution partnerships

**Language:** English first. Bilingual only if a specific audience segment requires it.

# Yeon Studios — Brand Guide

---

## Brand Overview

Yeon Studios is the infrastructure layer of a vertically integrated content and distribution ecosystem. It powers OTT platforms, video technology, and scalable distribution systems that enable brands and partners to launch, distribute, and monetise content at scale.

**Industry:** Media-tech and OTT infrastructure

**Strategic positioning:** Category-defining — not just a platform provider, but the backbone of an OTT-native content ecosystem where infrastructure, IP creation, and production are fully integrated, data-driven, and AI-augmented.

**Key differentiator:** Proprietary OTT and video infrastructure that gives clients tighter control over distribution, audience data, and monetisation — while operating in tandem with BeLive Studios' IP and content engine.

---

## Brand Voice & Tone

**How Yeon Studios sounds:** Strategic, visionary, and infrastructure-led.

- Speaks in systems and scale — not hype
- Positions as the enabler behind the content revolution
- Confident without being arrogant
- Future-facing without being vague
- Category-defining: introduces new language rather than borrowing competitors'

**Tone keywords:** Strategic · Visionary · Scalable · Authoritative · Precise

**Off-limits:** Political content, sensitive claims, unverified client results, competitor attacks, off-brand tone (casual, playful, sensational).

---

## Target Audience

**Primary targets (outreach):**
- Brand managers and marketing leads at mid-to-large companies
- Agency heads and media buying leads
- OTT platform operators and digital distributors
- Event organizers in media and tech
- Journalists and press covering media-tech and streaming
- Potential IP collaborators and content partners

**Market:**
- Primary: Singapore (strategic base and innovation hub)
- Secondary: Southeast Asia
- Expansion: Korea, China, global via IP and distribution partnerships

**Language:** English first. Bilingual only if a specific audience segment requires it.

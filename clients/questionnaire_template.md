Hi [CEO Name], I need your input to build our AI agent for Yeon Studios & BeLive Studios. Please answer all questions below:

## BRAND & BUSINESS

Q1. What are Yeon Studios and BeLive Studios — same business, separate brands, or parent/sub-brand?

Q2. What industry do we operate in? (live streaming, photography, events, media-tech, or mix?)

Q4. What is our key differentiator for each studio?

## CONTENT & SOCIAL MEDIA

Q5. Which platforms need managing? (Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, X, YouTube, Facebook — list all)

Q6. What type of content should we post? (thought leadership, promos, behind-the-scenes, case studies?)

Q7. How many posts per platform per week?

Q8. Do we have an existing content library the agent can repurpose?

Q9. What is our primary goal — grow followers, generate leads, get press coverage, speaking gigs, or industry partnerships? Please rank by priority.

Q10. Any competitors or brands we want to benchmark against?

## BRAND VOICE

Q12. Mobile app preference — web-based (faster) or native iOS/Android app?

Q13. Budget range and target timeline for this project?

Q14. What has been tried before for content or outreach — and what didn't work?

Q15. Who will review and approve content before it posts, or should the agent post autonomously?

Q17. Are there any topics, tones, or angles strictly off-limits for either brand?

Q18. How should each studio sound — formal and corporate, creative and bold, warm and approachable, or something else?

Q19. Do we have existing brand guidelines, a style guide, or past content that can set the tone?

## OUTREACH

Q20. Who are the key people we want to reach — journalists, brand managers, event organizers, agency heads?

Q21. Should the agent draft outbound outreach emails and DMs to new contacts — not just social posts?

Q22. What does success look like in 3 months? (e.g. X new followers, X press mentions, X leads generated)

## OPERATIONS & MARKET

Q23. What region/market are we targeting — Singapore only, Southeast Asia, or global?

Q24. What language(s) should content be written in? (English only, bilingual, etc.)

Q25. Who will be operating this agent day-to-day — Kevin, a social media manager, or the CEO?

Q26. Should the agent respond to inbound comments and DMs from followers, or only create and schedule outbound content?

Q27. Should the agent monitor industry news and suggest timely content based on trends?

Q28. Do we want analytics and performance reporting built into the app? (engagement, reach, follower growth)

Q29. Should the agent track outreach — who was contacted, reply status, follow-up reminders?

Q30. Are there any tools we already use that this must integrate with? (CRM, email, Notion, Google Sheets, etc.)

## TECHNICAL

Q28A. Do we have developer/API access for our social media accounts, or are we open to using a third-party scheduling tool that handles this?

Q28B. Should the agent generate visual content too (AI-generated images, graphics), or text captions only?
